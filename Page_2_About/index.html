Our Story

Founded in the heart of Chennai in 2005, Spice Junction began as a modest kitchen with a single vision — to bring the vibrant, unapologetic flavours of Indian cuisine to every plate. Our founder, Meena Ramaswamy, spent decades travelling across India, collecting recipes passed down through generations of home cooks who cared more about love than shortcuts.

Today, nearly two decades later, that same passion drives every dish we serve. Our chefs still grind their own masalas fresh each morning, source ingredients from trusted local farms, and cook over live flame — because great food cannot be rushed.

At Spice Junction, dining is not merely eating. It is an experience — of aroma, warmth, memory, and community. We invite you to pull up a chair and taste what we mean.
